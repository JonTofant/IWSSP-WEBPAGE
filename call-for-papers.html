Instructions for Paper Submission

1 Submit Your Paper

Authors are kindly invited to submit unpublished and original papers that are not under consideration for publication at other conferences or journals. The papers are required to contain original scientific, expert or applied results of the authors. The authors should submit papers that present applications of theoretical methods in solving engineering problems, new devices and/or systems, results of own experimental research, new approaches or solutions to existing problems, etc. All submitted papers are subject to review, but the authors are responsible for the originality and quality of their papers.

Papers must be submitted electronically by March 1, 2026 using EasyChair. Each paper will be blind reviewed by at least two independent reviewers and evaluated with special attention to its originality, significance, and clarity.

Go to EasyChair Submission

2 Camera-Ready Paper Submission

Revise Your Paper

Please read the reviews of your paper and follow the reviewers’ recommendations for revising it and preparing the camera-ready version according to the IWSSIP 2026 template (A4 format, up to five pages including figures and references). When you are done with the revision, please print out your camera-ready paper and carefully proofread it.

Download Word Template Download LaTeX Template

IEEE PDF eXpress

All conference articles must adhere to the IEEE Xplore PDF specification for compatibility. Therefore, authors are asked to carefully study IEEE PDF requirements and convert their camera-ready papers to PDF based on these requirements.

Creating your PDF eXpress Account (only for camera ready papers):

  • 1. Log in to the IEEE PDF eXpress Plus site.
  • 2. First-time users: Select the “New Users – Click Here” link.
  • 3. Enter the following:
    • TBD2026xp for the Conference ID (To be updated)
    • Your email address
    • A password
  • 4. An Online confirmation will be displayed and an email confirmation will be sent verifying your account setup.

Previous users of PDF eXpress or IEEE PDF eXpress Plus need to use the same password that was used for previous conferences, but should enter the new Conference ID. Please verify that your contact information remains valid.

Final Submission

To submit your final paper, please go to EasyChair, log in as an author, and upload your camera-ready paper. If you experience any problems during the upload process of your paper on EasyChair please contact info@iwssip2026.org.

IEEE Copyright Form

After uploading your camera-ready paper, you will need to submit your copyright form using the EasyChair electronic copyright mechanism. Please follow the instructions provided on the EasyChair submission page.
